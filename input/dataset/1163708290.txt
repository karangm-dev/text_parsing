Have you ever had deep fried pickles?  It is call Space Oddity here and is only $3.95 for 5 dill pickles spears coated in beer batter and deep fried to perfection.  It is served with sriracha ranch if you wish.  They also have other small plates like crispy chicken wings in asian glaze and spicy meatball sliders.  Of course they also have entrees.  Check out the menu at www.rocknoodles.com  Give it a try, you won't be sorry!  They have cozy seating inside or seating outside.  Delivery is also available.
